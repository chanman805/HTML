    Importing files isn't allowed in nuken Online Editor.

    We take your personal information very seriously. We have removed this feature from nuken Online Editor, as it poses a slight security threat. The chances of something bad happening to you via this method (of importing external HTML files) are extremely small, but rather than take that chance we have decided to drop the feature. We may allow users to import their own personal webpage files in a later update of nuken Online Editor, but for now, we invite you to download the offline versions of nuken; which all work as local files stored on your system or external storage device.
