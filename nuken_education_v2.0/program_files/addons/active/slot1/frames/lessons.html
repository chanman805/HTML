nuken: Your Lessons

Your educator has probably set you up with a collection of nuken Lessons. To access them, hover over the icon. You'll see a dropdown appear, with thumbnails for each lesson. Click on the desired lesson to load it into nuken!

Once the lesson has been loaded into nuken, you'll find that you can edit the code in the boxes freely. Follow your educator's instructions from this point onward (listed out in the nuken Lesson, or shared through conventional means).
