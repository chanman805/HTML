nuken: Exporting files

When you're finished with your assignment, just click the icon. It will automatically download your assignment as an HTML file to your default downloads location, which is usually a folder on your device named "Downloads" or "Files".


You'll notice a dropdown when you hover over the icon. This is where you can name your HTML file before you download it; or the option to "Preview File". This allows you to view your work in a stand-alone browser window.
